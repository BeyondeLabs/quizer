Quick User Guide

About

It is intended to make classroom revision easier and fun, both for the lecturers (tutors) and the students. Lecturers post revision questions of what they have taught in class and the students can test themselves. Lecturers can also easily communicate with their students by importing contacts from the app to their Google Contacts. Both students and lecturers can publish the revision questions to the Google Drive for offline print, archiving, etc.

The app also has a social-networking aspect in a way that both lecturers and students engage in side-line discussions alongside the review questions. The app is mainly intended for deployment on Google Apps for Education platform.

Google Docs App

The Google Docs (App Script) app publishes the revision question to your Google drive/docs. When you go to your Google Drive/Docs, you will see the published revision with the names Quizer: [title of the revision]

There is a Janitor app that runs at the end of every hour that takes all the revision apps and arranges them into one folder/collection called "Quizer"

NOTE: When you encounter any error while using this app, please relaunch it again (refresh your browser).

Google Contacts App

When you sync your students'/classmates' contacts, the app picks their details and creates contacts in your Google Contacts and puts all of them under one Contact Group called "QuizerApp".

NOTE: When you encounter any error while using this app, please relaunch it again (refresh your browser).
